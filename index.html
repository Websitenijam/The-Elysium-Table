Discover Egyptian Cuisine Reimagined

The Elysium Table was born from a passion for reviving authentic Egyptian culinary traditions with a modern twist. We bring the flavors that nourished pharaohs and pyramids into the 21st century.

Our elegant contemporary space pays homage to Egypt's golden age while our menu explores the country's rich culinary history along the Nile. You may think you know Egyptian food, but we have treasures yet to be uncovered.

At The Elysium Tables, we resurrect forgotten Egyptian ingredients and techniques, creating dishes fit for modern royalty.

Our Cuisine

"At The Elysium Table, each dish is a tribute to Egypt's rich culinary heritage — thoughtfully reimagined for the refined palate."

From Street to Royalty

From timeless street classics to royal-inspired creations, our menu bridges the flavors of tradition with the elegance of modern fine dining. Discover the evolution of Egyptian flavors through carefully composed dishes that honor both the past and present.

Crafted With Purpose

Every plate is crafted with intention, using authentic ingredients and elevated presentation to awaken the senses. Our chefs combine ancient techniques with contemporary creativity, transforming humble ingredients into refined culinary experiences.

A Sensory Journey

Let us transport you through Egypt's vibrant food culture — through spice markets, family tables, and ancient feasts — one thoughtfully composed course at a time. Our tasting menus tell the story of Egyptian cuisine across centuries and regions.

Our Promise

Our Mission

To be custodians of Egypt's culinary treasures while pioneering its contemporary expression. We source ethically, cook respectfully, and serve generously - bridging millennia of tradition with modern sensibilities. Every dish honors history while embracing innovation.

Our Vision

A world where Egyptian cuisine takes its rightful place at the global table. Where every spice tells a story, every technique connects generations, and every meal becomes a portal to the Nile's golden age. We dream of sustainable food systems inspired by ancient wisdom that nourish both body and soul.

Our Menu

Appetizers | المقبلات

Chef's Pick

Ful Medames - فول مدمس

Our signature dish - creamy mashed fava beans slow-cooked with garlic, premium olive oil, fresh lemon, and cumin. Garnished with heirloom tomatoes and parsley. The national dish of Egypt done right.

House Specialty

Shorbat Adas - شوربة عدس

Our award-winning velvety red lentil soup with garlic confit, caramelized carrots and onions, toasted cumin, and a hint of smoked paprika. Served with house-made croutons and a drizzle of truffle oil.

Baba Ghanoush - بابا غنوج

Roasted eggplant blended with tahini, garlic, lemon juice, and olive oil.

Warak Enab - ورق عنب

Grape leaves filled with rice, beef, herbs, and spices.

Salata Baladi - سلطة بلدي

Traditional Egyptian chopped salad of tomato, cucumber, onion, lemon juice, and olive oil.

Entrées | الأطباق الرئيسية

Signature Dish

Koshari - كشري

Egypt's beloved comfort food elevated - layers of perfectly cooked rice, lentils, macaroni and chickpeas under a crown of crispy onions and our signature spiced tomato sauce with a hint of garlic and cumin. Vegetarian yet deeply satisfying.

Customer Favorite

Molokhia - ملوخية

Our award-winning version of Egypt's famous jute leaf stew - slow-cooked with chicken broth and generous amounts of garlic, served with free-range chicken thighs and Egyptian short-grain rice. Silky texture with robust flavors passed down through generations.

Hawawshi - حواوشي

Spiced ground beef or lamb stuffed in Egyptian baladi bread and baked until golden and crisp.

Mahshi - محشي

Zucchini, peppers, or grape leaves filled with aromatic herbed rice and cooked in tomato broth.

Taameya - طعمية

Egyptian fava bean falafel blended with fresh herbs and spices, deep-fried to a crisp perfection.

Desserts | الحلويات

Roz Bel Laban - رز باللبن

Creamy pudding of rice, milk, and sugar, delicately flavored with rose water or vanilla.

Basbousa - بسبوسة

A soft semolina cake soaked in sugar syrup, often topped with almonds or coconut flakes.

Umm Ali - أم علي

Warm dessert of flaky pastry with milk, nuts, and raisins.

Feteer Meshaltet — فطير مشلتت

A buttery, flaky pastry served warm with honey or jams.

Qatayef - قطايف

Stuffed sweet pancakes filled with nuts or cream, folded and sweetened with syrup.

Beverages | المشروبات

Best-Seller

Karkade - كركديه

A tangy, floral tea served hot or cold — refreshing and naturally caffeine-free.

Lemon bi Na'na' - ليمون بالنعناع

A zesty lemon drink blended with fresh mint and honey.

Sobia - سوبيا

Chilled coconut milk drink with sugar and vanilla.

Shai bi Na'na' - شاي بالنعناع

Fragrant black tea infused with fresh mint leaves. Served hot or iced.

Tamer Hindi - تمر هندي

Tangy tamarind drink sweetened and chilled.

International Society of Egyptian Gastronomy

Golden Ankh Award 2023

Our chef's mastery of age-old techniques blended with modern sensibilities preserves the legacy of pharaonic kitchens while delighting contemporary diners.
